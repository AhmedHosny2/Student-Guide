Easy Weighted Grade Calculator

hi, I'm vee, and welcome to the Grade Calculator! Let me guide you through it: Quickly determine your total weighted grade by providing exam grades, your performance, and weights. Receive instant results and discover the minimum grade required to pass. Simplify your academic assessment with this user-friendly tool.

  • Name (nameInput): Enter the name of the exam or assessment (e.g., midterm).Example: "midterm"
  • Weight (weightInput): Assign a weight to the exam, indicating its importance as a percentage. Example: "30%" signifies 30% of the total grade.
  • Exam Grade (examInput): Input the raw score for the exam, typically out of 100. Example: "85" if the raw score is 85 out of 100.
  • Your Grade (gradeInput): Input your individual performance grade for the exam. Example: "85" if your achieved grade is 85.
